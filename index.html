Scholar GitHub CV

I focus on integrating Camera, LiDAR and other sensor data to enhance perception in autonomous multi-robot systems. Key contributions include information-fusion architectures and pipelines that improve object detection, tracking, and situational awareness in cluttered, dynamic environments, with applications to search-and-rescue.

You can get in touch with me at a.nanjaiyalatha [at] utwente [dot] nl.

I also write blog posts on academic topics sometimes, which you can find in ஓய்வு. It means "leisure" or "rest" in Tamil.
