I am a Doctoral Candidate in Computer Science at the University of Twente, The Netherlands. My research focuses on integrating Camera, LiDAR and other sensor data to enhance perception in autonomous multi-robot systems. Key contributions include developing information fusion architecture and pipelines that improve Object Detection, Tracking, collision avoidance, situational awareness in cluttered dynamic environments with applications to search-and-rescue scenarios.

You can get in touch with me at a.nanjaiyalatha [at] utwente [dot] nl. You can also find me on Google Scholar and GitHub.

I also write blog posts on academic topics sometimes, which you can find in ஓய்வு.
